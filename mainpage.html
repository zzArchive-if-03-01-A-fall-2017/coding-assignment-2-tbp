Sports

Introduction

On this webpage we want to show you some different types of sports and make you familiar with them.

Sport is an activity involving physical, mind, motorised, co-ordination or animal-supported exertion and skill in which an individual or team competes against another or others for entertainment and money.

Sports should have the following criteria:

  1. have an element of competition
  2. be in no way harmful to any living creature
  3. not rely on equipment provided by a single supplier
  4. not rely on any "luck" element specifically designed into the sport

Couch potato

What is a couch potato?

A couch potato is a person who is often considered lazy or sometimes even overweight.
They only watch their favourite series, films and etc. or play games all day long.
Couch potatoes try to avoid getting out of the house. They only go out to buy food or to go to work.


Tips & Tricks

If you don't want to get one or if u already are one, here you can find some tips which can help you to come out of this zone or to help you not getting one.


  1. Set a Goal !. Decide how much TV is the right amount for you. It might be one hour a day or five hours a week. Make sure the goal is reasonable and within your control.
  2. Start eating healthy food. One of the most important things in life are your eating habbits.
  3. Meet friends or get some if you don't have any.
  4. Get yourself a job or a new hobby.
